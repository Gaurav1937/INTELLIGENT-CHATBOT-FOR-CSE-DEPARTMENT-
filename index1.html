ABOUT US

The CSE department is a hub of innovation, driven by a mission to provide top-tier education and cutting-edge research. Our vision is to empower students with the skills needed to thrive in the dynamic field of computer science. Our distinguished faculty, experts in AI, machine learning, software engineering, cybersecurity, and data science, bring a wealth of knowledge and industry experience. Committed to excellence, we foster an environment that nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and a passion for learning, preparing our students for success in the ever-evolving tech landscape

Programs Offered

Our Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Department offers a range of undergraduate programs, including B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering, B.Tech CSBS (Computer Science and Business Systems), and B.Tech CSE (Hons.) with specializations in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, Internet of Things & Cybersecurity, and Hindi. Additionally, the postgraduate program, M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering, provides advanced studies and research opportunities.
